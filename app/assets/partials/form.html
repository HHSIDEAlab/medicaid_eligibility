Welcome to MAGI in the Cloud

As of March 1, 2015, the MAGI Software will no longer be publicly supported except to NESCSO members. If you are a NESCSO member, please choose your organization. If you are not a NESCSO member, please see the notice below.



The MAGI Software is hosted by NESCSO, a nonprofit membership organization that provides services on a shared basis, including advisory, analytical, consulting and systems development services, to public entities furnishing services under Title XIX of the Social Security Act (the "Medicaid Program").

The MAGI Software was developed by BlueLabs, LLC, through grant funding from the State Health Reform Assistance Network, a Robert Wood Johnson Foundation funded program, and under the direction of the CMS Entrepreneurs Program.

NESCSO, in concert with CMS, has determined that the MAGI Software is a cost-effective solution for public entities in determining eligibility for the Medicaid Program. States can access the MAGI Software by becoming a NESCSO member, and in so doing will also be able to access certain technical consulting and support services available pursuant to the terms of the Membership Agreement. You can download a copy of the Membership Agreement by clicking here. If you have any questions about becoming a member or the Membership Agreement, please email Brenda.Harvey@umassmed.edu.
